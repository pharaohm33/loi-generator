# Version 1.0

**Released:** 2026-07-10

## What's Included

Full launch of the Suede Buffalo LLC Seller Carryback Deal Calculator & LOI Generator as a single-file GitHub Pages web app.

---

## Seller Carry Payment Modes (5 total)

| Mode | Description |
|---|---|
| Deferred-Simple | Flat $/mo principal; interest accrues on declining balance → paid at balloon |
| Interest-Only (IO) | Monthly interest payments; full principal due at balloon |
| P&I (Amortizing) | Standard amortizing P&I; balloon = remaining balance at term end |
| Deferred-To-Exit | $0/mo; all principal + accrued simple interest due at balloon |
| Deferred-Then-Pay | Deferral period ($0/mo, interest accrues), then IO payments; deferred interest rolls to balloon |

---

## Calculator Features

- **Income & NOI** — Direct NOI override, rent-based inputs, market cap rate with click-to-copy implied valuation
- **Proforma NOI** — Linear ramp from current → proforma NOI over stabilization months, then NOI growth %/yr
- **% NOI Increase Per Year** — compounds NOI in exit grid and buyer presentation
- **Deal Pricing** — Purchase price, cash at closing, as-is value, ARV, rehab budget
- **Senior Loan** — DSCR and Hard Money toggle; loan sized on as-is value; binding constraint badge (LTV / LTC / ARV%)
- **Seller Carry Note** — All 5 payment modes with mode-specific input fields
- **Closing Costs** — 1st escrow (% contract), 2nd escrow (% as-is), acq/bird dog fee (% purchase price), points, broker, EMD, TAF fee
- **Cash Back to Buyer** — Full waterfall with park-in-escrow toggle
- **Exit Analysis** — Year-by-year grid through balloon+3 years; viability badges; proforma Sale+CF column

## LOI Generator

- Bold section headers copy cleanly into Google Docs
- **Non-Exclusive LOI** (no buyer company) — includes non-circumvent and non-exclusive clauses
- **LOI** (buyer company filled) — clean deal terms, lists buyer company, no circumvent language
- 0% interest rate produces principal-only language (no interest references)
- All 5 payment modes generate accurate, mode-specific language

## Buyer Presentation

- Every dollar figure shown as % of purchase price
- As-is cap rate, LTV%, DSCR ratios (senior only and all debt)
- Proforma NOI section with ramp path, DSCR at stabilization, DSCR at balloon
- Current NOI exit + proforma exit (cap-rate implied value)
- Auto-updating disclaimer based on active paths

## Other

- **URL sharing** — all inputs encoded into URL; shareable with partners
- **Admin Template** — one-click fills Suede Buffalo LLC defaults (Don Ava Capital LLC as buyer, 75% LTV, 7% rate, 2.5% TAF, etc.)
