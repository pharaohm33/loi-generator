# Version 1.1

**Released:** 2026-07-10
**Previous version:** v1.0

---

## What Changed

### New: IO + Deferred Simple Payment Mode

Added a 6th seller carry payment mode for deals where the seller wants two separate interest streams — one paid monthly and one deferred to the balloon.

**Use case:** A seller asks for 4% IO payments monthly *plus* 4% simple interest that accrues on the full principal and is paid at exit. Total effective interest = 8%, split across two buckets.

**How it works:**
- Set `sc_rate` = IO rate (e.g. 4%) — this is the monthly payment rate
- Set `Deferred Simple Interest Rate` = deferred rate (e.g. 4%) — this accrues annually on full principal
- Monthly payment = `carry × io_rate / 12` (IO only)
- Balloon = `carry + carry × deferred_rate × term_years`
- Exit grid `Seller Owed` column grows year-by-year as deferred interest accrues

**LOI output (SELLER RECEIVES block):**
```
Monthly Payment Interest Rate: 4.00% simple interest
Payment Type: 4% IO + 4% Deferred Simple | 8% total simple interest
Term: 10 Years
```

**LOI narrative** clearly distinguishes the two streams: IO portion paid monthly, deferred portion due at balloon.

---

### Updated: LOI "Interest Rate" → "Monthly Payment Interest Rate"

In the SELLER RECEIVES section, the label `Interest Rate:` has been renamed to `Monthly Payment Interest Rate:` across all payment modes.

**Before (v1.0):**
```
Interest Rate: 7.00% simple interest
```

**After (v1.1):**
```
Monthly Payment Interest Rate: 7.00% simple interest
```

**Why:** Eliminates seller confusion — makes it explicit that the rate shown is what drives the monthly payment, not the total interest cost of the deal (which may include deferred components).

## Full Mode Table (v1.1 — 6 modes)

| Mode | Monthly Payment | Balloon |
|---|---|---|
| Deferred-Simple | Flat $/mo principal | Remaining principal + accrued simple interest |
| Interest-Only (IO) | `carry × rate / 12` | Full principal |
| P&I (Amortizing) | Standard amortizing P&I | Remaining balance at term end |
| Deferred-To-Exit | $0 | Principal + all accrued simple interest |
| Deferred-Then-Pay | $0 during deferral, then IO | Principal + deferred interest |
| **IO + Deferred Simple** *(new)* | IO on io_rate | Principal + `carry × deferred_rate × term` |
